Continuous-time output

Exact posterior expectations without discretization bias.

PDMPSamplers implements piecewise deterministic Markov process samplers for Bayesian inference. These samplers produce continuous-time output and can exploit subsampled gradients for scalability.

Available for both Julia and R.
